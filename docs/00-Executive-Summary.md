# ORION Blueprint v0.1-alpha — Executive Summary

Orion is not an English learning application.

Orion is a Mastery Engine.

The first validated implementation focuses on Spoken English, but the engine is designed to support any domain in which a learner progresses from exposure to automatic performance through guided practice and measurable mastery.

## Product Mission
Provide every learner with a personal digital coach capable of guiding deliberate practice until measurable mastery is achieved.

The platform complements teachers. It never replaces them.

## MVP Objective
The MVP validates the engine through Spoken English.

The objective is not to prove speech recognition. Speech Recognition is replaceable infrastructure.

The objective is to prove structured mastery practice: Discover → Practice → Master Challenge → Final Exam.

## Core IP
- Mastery Engine
- Mission Engine
- Assessment Engine
- Coach Experience
- Mission Builder
